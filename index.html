01 — The Challenge

The Problem
with Traditional Car Wash

Time Wasted

Busy professionals and families lose hours driving to car wash stations, waiting in queues, and returning — time that could be spent on what matters.

No Flexibility

Traditional services operate on fixed schedules and locations. Elderly people and those with mobility challenges find it especially difficult to access them.

Quality Gap

Generic car washes use harsh chemicals and rushed processes that damage paint, leave water spots, and deliver inconsistent results.

Water Waste

Conventional car wash stations consume up to 300 liters per vehicle — environmentally irresponsible and increasingly costly as water scarcity grows.

"Moroccan car owners lose an average of 3+ hours per month on car maintenance visits."
